Adventure Game!

Introduction

Adventure Game! is a text-based choose-your-own-adventure game that hearkens back to the 1980s. You know what that means: use your imagination to make the adventure come to life!

Instructions

To play the game, follow these steps:
  • Read the paragraph.
  • Read the options below the paragraph.
  • Click the option you want to take.
  • Read the resulting paragraph.
  • Repeat!
Once you reach the end of the game, click the Start Over button to play again.

Let the adventure begin!

You wake up and there's a book on your chest. The book wasn't there when you went to sleep. Puzzled, you look around, but no one is in your room. "There's no way someone could have gotten into my house," you say aloud. You open the book to try to find a clue about how the book ended up here.
Choose an option to move forward.
